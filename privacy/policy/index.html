Privacy Policy_

This privacy policy is different.

Others make you sign away your data with fancy language.

This just explains what parts of your data I collect, why I do it, and how I protect it in plain english.

Data I Collect

Every time you visit my website (any website, really), the server adds a line to its log file. It typically looks like this:

108.162.242.45 - - [02/May/2022:13:17:27 +0000] "GET / HTTP/1.1" 200 7218 "-" "Mozilla/5.0 (X11; Linux x86_64) AppleWebKit/537.36 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/100.0.4896.127 Safari/537.36"

Some parts of the line are just technical records like which version of the HTTP protocol your browser is using, the exact webpage your browser is requesting, and so on. There are three main pieces of non-technical data collected.

  • 108.162.242.45 - This is your IP address, which tells the server where to send the webpage. It can identify your rough location (within a few blocks), but changes often.
  • 02/May/2022:13:17:27 +0000 - This is the exact time your browser sent the request to view the website.
  • Mozilla/5.0 (X11; Linux x86_64) AppleWebKit/537.36 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/100.0.4896.127 Safari/537.36 - Although it may look confusing, this part identifies your browser and its exact version, as well as your operating system.
Why It's Collected

Most websites collect your data in order to show you more targeted ads, or sell them to advertising companies who will then use your data to do the same. This website isn't like that. I will only ever use your data to:

  • Analyse visitor statistics - For example, I can use the data to see when my website gets visitors and which countries they are from to create better content.
  • Diagnose technical errors - When the server crashes, I may analyse the logs to see why it crashedâ€”sometimes, specific browsers can make websites crash.
  • Prevent technical abuse - Sometimes, website come under spam attacks. Having logs of which IP addresses access a website can allow me to block spammers.
How It's Protected

The server containing the data (and the website) is completely encrypted with industry-standard encryption methods. Log files are also securely deleted 1 month after their generation date to conserve disk space. Finally, attackers would have a very difficult time extracting personal information from the few pieces of data I collect. I will also never sell or transfer your data to others unless required by law.

Other Facts to Note
  • If you are still concerned about your IP address being collected, you can use a VPN to completely change it.
  • If you are concerned about your request times being collected, simply access the website When you would normally be asleep.
  • If you are concerned about your browser version being collected, the Tor Browser allows you to minic other browsers.
  • If you have other concerns or suggestions, feel free to drop me a line via the contact methods at the bottom of this page.
